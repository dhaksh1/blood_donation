About Blood Unity

Blood Unity is a comprehensive web application designed to facilitate blood donation and connect donors with recipients. Our mission is to connect donors and recipients efficiently while promoting the importance of regular blood donations for a healthier society.

Blood donation

"Blood donation is a lifesaving act that everyone can do. Donors can usually give blood every 8 to 12 weeks, giving their bodies time to recover. Your donation can make a significant impact on someone's life, from accident victims to patients undergoing surgery. Consider becoming a regular donor to ensure a stable blood supply for emergencies and medical treatments. Your generosity can bring hope and healing to those in need." Choose Your Blood Type

Pre donation instructions

Get enough sleep on the night of the donation.

Eat a balanced meal two hours before donating.

You should not drink liquids with caffeine.

You should drink more fluids than usual.

Donation instructions

Go to the reception

The donor starts their journey by contacting the registry.

Take blood sample

Take a blood sample and test to determine whether or not to donate.

Doctor's permit

The doctor makes a decision about accepting a blood donation.

Food

Before donating it is necessary to eat a meal, do not donate blood on an empty stomach.

Blood donation

The donation is made in the donation room.

Donor's certificate

After donating, the donor's certificate will be issued.

Post donation instructions

You should drink more fluids than usual.

After donating, take a break and snack.

After 10-15 minutes you can leave the place.

Avoid heavy physical activity for 5 hours.
